What is JEET?

JEET is an AI-powered web application that predicts the likelihood of students dropping out after Class 12, specifically those preparing for India’s JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) and NEET (National Entrance cum Eligibility Test).

Why is JEET necessary?

JEET was designed to address some of the big problems that students give into without knowing:

Students entering Engineering and Medical with no real interest in the fields.
JEE and NEET being time-intensive exams which the students can invest in other fields where they can develop themselves better
Absence of an early-warning system to identify struggling students before they face burnout or dropout.

These challenges highlight the urgent need for a tool that doesn’t just react after failure but actively guides students early on. JEET steps in as that safety net, giving clarity before years of effort are lost.

When should you use JEET?

JEET is most effective when used early in your academic journey. The earlier you know your risks, the more time you have to choose the right path and succeed.

✔️ Before joining JEE/NEET coaching to see if it truly fits your goals.
✔️ After Class 10, when deciding whether to take the science stream.
✔️ During Class 11 & 12 preparation to check if you are at risk of burnout or dropout.
✔️ If you feel uncertain about your interest in engineering/medical careers.

Using JEET at the right time can save years of effort and guide you toward a career that matches your strengths and interests.

How does JEET work?

1️⃣ Data Preprocessing — SMOTE balancing, scaling, and pipelines
2️⃣ Model Training — Voting Classifier (Random Forest + SVC)
3️⃣ Explainability — SHAP values for feature importance
4️⃣ Deployment — Streamlit app + Gemini integration
